Android Lunch Kit

This Android Lunch Kit provides a way to take lunch on the go! No more messy ice packs, leaking bags and soggy sandwiches!

About

Android Lunch Kit is a blue Bugdroid-branded lunch box. The lid contains a small compartment for the included utensils and the main container has three seperate areas for food.

Contains

  • One plastic spork and knife
