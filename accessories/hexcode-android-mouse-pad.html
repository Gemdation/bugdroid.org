Hexcode Mouse Pad Cloth

Featuring the popular ‘hexcode” Android design from the mini collectibles series.

What is it?

The Hexcode Android Mouse Pad is decorated version of the cloth mouse pad, featuring green-bordered black Bugdroid with green hexagons. It is a mouse pad with a cloth surface and a non-slip foam rubber back.

What does it have?

  • One mouse pad (7½" x 8¾")

Where can I buy it?

This item is from the Android Foundry and is currently not listed anywhere else.
