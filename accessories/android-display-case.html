Android Display Case

About

The Android Display Case is a protective figurine display case for the figurines. They offer UV protection for the figurines and can stack/attach to other cases.

It appears that the bases were also available in white.

View original Dead Zebra Shop synopsis

These display cases are engineered specifically with the collector in mind! Each base is designed to fit snugly with other bases, subtly tapered tops allow for easy stacking. Create a custom and flexible display for your Android collectibles, Dunnys, Be@rbricks or other mini figures!

The high quality clear acrylic tops keep your collectibles dust free, while filtering out 60% of the UV rays that can degrade the materials and paint colors of your collectibles. There’s no big ugly circular injection point on the top of these cases, we moved the injection points off to the side and made them virtually invisible!
