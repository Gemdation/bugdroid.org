Hidden Android Decal

Peeking around a corner or out from a door, the Hidden Android is ready to greet you with a friendly wave. This single piece wall decal is easy to apply and sure to amuse.

What is it?

Android Hidden Decal is a huge wall decal with classic Bugdroid waving, it has repositional arms and is reusable all together. It is green with a white outline, excluding the bottom edge.

What does it have?

  • One decal (22″x14″)

Where can I buy it?
