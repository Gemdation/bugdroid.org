Android Iconic Sticker

About

The Android Iconic Sticker Sheet is a sticker sheet featuring the 2019 Bugdroid branding. Ten are droids facing different ways, eight are normal logos, two are big wordmarks, three are small wordmarks, and finally, four are the regular Iconic logos.
