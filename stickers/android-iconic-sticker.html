Android Iconic Sticker

Android everything! Now you can brand all your devices and items with this Google sticker sheet featuring the Android logo and brand name.

What is it?

The Android Iconic Sticker Sheet is a sticker sheet featuring the 2019 Bugdroid branding. Ten are droids facing different ways, eight are normal logos, two are big wordmarks, three are small wordmarks, and finally, four are the regular Iconic logos.

What does it have?

  • One sheet of 27 stickers

Where can I buy it?

This item was exclusively on the Google Merchandise Store and had since been taken off.
