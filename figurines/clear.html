Clear

Clearly something is special about this Android.. Pop off his head and you have yourself a micro Android!

This little guy has a built-in keychain attachment and features an articulated head and arms, and legs that move enough to let him sit up unaided.

About

The Clear figure is a transparent Bugdroid, containing a much smaller classic Bugdroid inside. The large clear bot can be opened, and the micro bot has a keychain attachment and can be stood up.

This figurine was apart of Series 03, released in September 19, 2012.

Contains

  • Two figures
  • One windowed box
