Standard D.I.Y. MEGA Edition

Android mini collectibles not enough Android for you? Each MEGA Android towers almost 10" tall! These massive fellows feature a rotating and removable head, expressive ball jointed arms, and a special double wall internal liner. Unlock the head to reveal an internal chamber big enough to hold a can of your favorite tasty beverage...or anything else you might want to hide in there!

Available in the iconic Android Green, or ready for your own artistic touch in a blank Do It Yourself edition.

About

The Standard D.I.Y. MEGA Edition figure is a large white Bugdroid that can open up to reveal an insulating soda container. Unlike the original Standard MEGA Edition, this one has removable limbs for ease of decorating.

In an archived snapshot, the details on the figurine say "9¾" tall, 8½" wide Vinyl and ABS!".

  • One window box
