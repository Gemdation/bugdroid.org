About

The Standard Edition figure is classic Bugdroid with poseable arms and a wordmark on its behind, setting the standard for the rest of the figurines.

First three images are of the 2014 re-release, the last two are of the original Series 01 release.

This was the first one of Series 1, and the first one entirely.

Contains

  • One (window) box
  • One carrier box (large quantity orders)
