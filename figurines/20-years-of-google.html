20 Years of Google

We have a limited stock of these Special Edition Androids commemorating 20 years of Google! Originally released to Google staff in 2018, we have some available direct for collectors! The 20 Year Edition features a colorful design, removable birthday hat and balloon accessory on a poseable wire!

About

The 20 Years of Google figure is a multi-colored Bugdroid with a party hat and balloon designed for Google's 20th anniversary.

These were originally exclusive to Google employess in 2018 but were later on released to the public.

  • One figure
  • One window box
