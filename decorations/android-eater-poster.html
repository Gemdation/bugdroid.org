Android Eater Poster

The Android codename that never was! “Key Lime Pie” was long rumored to be the codename for Android’s next big update. Alas, “Key Lime Pie” was gobbled up, making way for the newly announced Kit Kat!

This colorful poster designed by Andrew Bell features an Android polishing off a helping of doomed Key Lime Pie, surrounded by former codename treats!

About

Android Eater is poster featuring a large monster-esque Bugdroid chomping at thje Android version desserts. Since there is no lollipop, it was probably created before Android 5.0. The poster is also semi-gloss covered and had thick cardstock.
