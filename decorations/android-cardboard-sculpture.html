Android Cardboard Sculpture

A fun new design for a simple desk novelty. The Android sculpture is completely customizable and fun to assemble.

What is it?

Android Cardboard Sculpture is a cardboard statue of the classic Bugdroid. It is built by assembling pieces from three sheets of cardboard.

What does it have?

  • Three cardboard sheets

Where can I buy it?

This item was exclusively on the Google Merchandise Store and had since been taken off.
