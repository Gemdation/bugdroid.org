Candy Cane Android Cardboard Sculpture

About

Candy Cane Android Cardboard Sculpture is a cardboard statue of the classic Bugdroid, but with a decorated body of Candy Canes finished with a red bow. It is built by assembling pieces from three sheets of cardboard.
