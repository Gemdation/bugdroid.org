Snowflake Android Cardboard Sculpture

Enjoy putting together this fun winter themed Android cardboard sculpture.

What is it?

Snowflake Android Cardboard Sculpture is a cardboard statue of the classic Bugdroid, but with a decorated body of snowflakes finished with a red bow. It is built by assembling pieces from three sheets of cardboard.

What does it have?

  • Three cardboard sheets
  • One "bow" paper piece

Where can I buy it?

This item was exclusively on the Google Merchandise Store and had since been taken off.
