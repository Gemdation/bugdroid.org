Android Puzzlebot V2

Assemble your very own Android robot to display on your desk.

About

The Android Puzzlebot V2 is a tiny Bugdroid brick statue made out of not-LEGO pieces, it has a propeller hat while standing on a black platform. The "V2" part of the name suggests it is a revision of another brick statue.

Contains

  • One brick set (109 pieces)
  • Instructions
