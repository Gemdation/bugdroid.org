Bugdroid Buddy

About

Bugdroid Buddy is a little involving an interactive Bugdroid to play with. The bot can tap dance, fly, sleep, and more.

Background color can be custom set and it can be set as your smartphone's live wallpaper. The game is very reminiscent of Talking Tom.
