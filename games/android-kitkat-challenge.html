Android KitKat Challenge

About

Android KitKat Challenge is an action game themed around the Android KitKat promotional videos and inspired by the popular Dumb Ways to Die series. Various mini challenges such as cut a KitKat, spot the Droid with the KitKat, tap something, etc. And while playing the timer will get shorter over time. The game also has 3 extra wallpapers.
