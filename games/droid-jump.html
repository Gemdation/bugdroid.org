Droid Jump

About

Droid Jump is an endless jumper game developed by ElectricSheep/Patrick Bouliou. Bugdroid is jumping through the sky with eclair platforms, avoiding obstacles.

This game is the predecessor to Extreme Droid Jump. Outdated Notice: Gameplay does not work on newer Android versions.
