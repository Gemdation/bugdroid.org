Android Intro Compilation

This is a compilation of video intros themed around Android.

Intro 1: A silver Bugdroid walks between the half of the screen, and turns green when it gets to the other half. Realizing that the half changes its color, it goes in the middle.

Intro 2: A Bugdroid head is seen walking with its antennas, then a body grows and it sits upright. The arms and legs then grow as well. A 2007 Android wordmark appears and the bot look at it.

Intro 3: A green pill-shaped body is seen rolling, it then gets small, around the size of a Bugdroid head. The 3D animation then goes 2D and eyes and antennas appear.

Intro 4: A Bugdroid head falls from above and grows a body, eyes, and limbs just as fast. It waves at the camera, ending the compilation.
