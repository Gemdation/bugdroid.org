Android Logo Evolution

This is an 'evolution' animation video of most of the Android dessert icons, transitioning with fluid animations. It goes from Android 1.5 Cupcake to Android 9 Pie.

Ironically, the uploader's question at the end of the video was unaware that the dessert naming scheme would be later on dropped by Android 10 Q.
