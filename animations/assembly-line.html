Assembly Line

This video takes place in a smartphone factory. Bugdroids are assmebled from body, arms, legs, head, and eyes. Once they are done, all bots go to a conveyor belt with smartphones. Each Bugdroid jumps in to one phone, making it an Android phone.

The creator later re-released this animation as a YouTube Short in 2023, with some minor changes.
