Product Example

As touching as a text message is, nothing beats a hand-written note! These new Android Foundry notepad, paper, card and sticker sets are perfect for the job. Go fly a kite with the fun "Andy's Day Out" set featuring an adventuring classic green Android!

What is it?

Andy's Day Out is a set of papers, and notepads themed around Bugdroid having fun! There is even a sticker sheet along with green envelopes.

What does it have?

  • 4 notecards (4"x6")
  • 4 envelopes
  • 2 notepads featuring 2 designs (4.5"x7.25")
  • 8 full size sheets featuring 4 different designs (8.5"x11")
  • Sheet of 7 stickers

This is currently available at one online store.
