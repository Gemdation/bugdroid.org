Android Pride Pin

Add some personality to your clothing or bag with this rainbow Android pin, in celebration of Pride.

What is it?

The Android Pride Pin is a colorful stainless steel pin with the new Bugdroid head.

What does it have?

  • One pin (1.18" x 0.71")

Where can I buy it?

This item is available on the 'Everything Else' side of the Google Merchandise Store, not including the United States and Canada.
