Android RFID Journal

Google promotes data security, and this Android journal is another way you can keep your data safe from remote RFID scanning.

About

The Android RFID Journal is a black journal with a Bugdroid embosses on the cover, this journal is resistant to unwanted RFID scanning. The notebook is enclosed with a zipper instead of a spine or spiral.
