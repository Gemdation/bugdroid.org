Businesstime

As touching as a text message is, nothing beats a hand-written note! These new Android Foundry notepad, paper, card and sticker sets are perfect for the job. Get to work with the "Business Time" design set featuring some properly attired Androids on the job!

What is it?

Businesstime is the professional version of Andy's Day Out. Containing the same amount of things, this will be perfect for your colleague! Just...make sure they don't use iPhone.

What does it have?

  • 4 notecards (4"x6")
  • 4 envelopes
  • 2 notepads featuring 2 designs (4.5"x7.25")
  • 8 full size sheets featuring 4 different designs (8.5"x11")
  • Sheet of 7 stickers
