Android Chroma Bottle

Perfect for staying hydrated, this single wall Android bottle is ideal for hikes or travelling. It’s light and portable so it won’t weigh you down while you’re on the go.

What is it?

The Android Chroma Bottle is a black bottle with the modern Android branding on its side, it has a single twist-off lid. Similar to the international Android Bottle.

What does it have?

  • One bottle

Where can I buy it?

This item is available on the 'Everything Else' side of the Google Merchandise Store for £13. This is not including the United States and Canada.
